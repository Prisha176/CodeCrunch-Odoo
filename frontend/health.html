Stay Hydrated

Aim for 8-10 glasses of water daily to maintain energy levels and support metabolic functions.

Why is this important? 🤔

  • 💧 Improves skin health and hydration.
  • ⚡ Boosts energy and reduces fatigue.
  • 🩺 Supports kidney function and detoxification.

Fun Fact! 😄

Did you know? Your brain is about 75% water! Staying hydrated helps you think clearly and stay focused.
