Walking Alone at Night

If you feel unsafe while walking alone at night, stay in well-lit areas and avoid isolated paths. Carry a personal safety alarm and share your location with a trusted contact.

Detailed Advice

Here are some additional steps you can take:

  • Always be aware of your surroundings.
  • Use apps like Guardian Mode to share your live location with friends or family.
  • Carry a whistle or personal safety device.
