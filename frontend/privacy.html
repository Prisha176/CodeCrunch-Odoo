Privacy Policy

SW

Introduction

Welcome to One Stop Woman, your dedicated platform for empowering women through education, community support, and professional growth. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details when you register or interact with our platform.
  • Profile Information: Details such as your profession, interests, and preferences to personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our platform, including pages visited, features used, and interactions with other users.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for technical and security purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services, including community features, job boards, and event listings.
  • Personalize your experience by recommending relevant communities, events, and resources.
  • Communicate with you about updates, new features, and opportunities tailored to your interests.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform by monitoring usage and preventing misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information with other users or third parties.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our platform, such as hosting providers and analytics services.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Data Security

We take your security seriously. We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These include encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
