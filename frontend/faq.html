Frequently Asked Questions

SW
1. How do I join a community?

To join a community, navigate to the "Communities" section, search for the community you're interested in, and click the "Join" button. You'll need to create an account if you don't already have one.

2. How can I contact a consultant?

You can contact a consultant by visiting the "Consultants" section, selecting the consultant you want to connect with, and clicking the "Chat" or "Video Call" button.

3. What is Guardian Mode?

Guardian Mode is a safety feature that allows you to share your location with trusted contacts in real-time. It's designed to ensure your safety when traveling or attending events.

4. How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and you'll receive a link to reset your password.

5. Can I create my own community?

Yes, you can create your own community! Navigate to the "Communities" section and click on "Create Community." Fill out the required details, and your community will be live once approved.
